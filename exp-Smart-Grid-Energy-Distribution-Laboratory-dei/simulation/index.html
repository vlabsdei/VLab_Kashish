Smart Grid Energy Distribution Formulas

Total Generated Power = Solar Generation + Wind Generation

Total Demand = Residential Load + Commercial Load + Industrial Load

Surplus Power = Generated Power − Total Demand

Grid Efficiency = (Min(Total Generated Power, Total Demand) ÷ Total Demand) × 100

Renewable Utilization = (Total Demand ÷ Total Generated Power) × 100

Grid Condition:

• Surplus Power → Generated Power > Demand

• Balanced → Generated Power = Demand

• Power Deficit → Generated Power < Demand
